# HR Cloud Settings Company Structure

Use this topic for company profile data, organizational structure, employment types, and status setup.

## Company Information

- Company name, logo, and brand colors are used across the system.
- Time zone, date format, and currency are set here.
- Company anniversary date can be stored.
- Company description can appear in Workmates profile areas.

## Organizational Structure

- **Positions** are job titles reused across People, Recruit, and Onboard.
- **Departments** group employees into business units.
- **Divisions** are optional higher-level groupings above departments.
- **Locations** represent physical office or work locations.
- **Employment Types** define classifications such as Full-time, Part-time, or Contractor.

## Employment Status Types

- Active, Inactive, Prehire, On Leave, and other status types are managed here.
- Admins can add custom status types.
- Status transitions depend on these definitions.
