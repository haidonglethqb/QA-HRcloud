# Recruit Hiring and Communication

Use this topic for offer letters, hiring conversion, communications, interviews, and applicant record handling.

## Hiring an Applicant

### Method A: With Offer Letter

1. Move applicant to `Offer` stage.
2. Send offer letter with `Send Offer Letter`.
3. Applicant receives email and signs electronically.
4. Admin clicks `Hire` to convert the applicant into an employee in People.

### Method B: Without Offer Letter

1. Move applicant to final stage.
2. Click `Hire` directly.
3. Enter hire details to create the employee record.

If the person was previously a terminated employee, the hire flow must use rehire behavior.

## Offer Letters

- Offer letters use customizable templates with merge fields.
- Applicants sign electronically.
- Signed offers can be downloaded as PDF.
- Offers can be revoked and can show custom confirmation messages.

## Communication

- Recruiters can send emails from within Recruit.
- Email templates can vary by status change.
- Email Forwarding can auto-import applications from a mailbox alias.
- SMS templates require Twilio integration.
- Email alerts notify recruiters about applicant changes.

## Interview Scheduling

- Interviews can be scheduled from the applicant profile.
- Google Calendar and Office 365 integrations are supported.

## Document Privacy and Retention

- Uploaded application documents can have privacy restrictions.
- Applicant data retention can be configured for rejected or expired candidates.
- GDPR compliance behavior depends on those retention settings.

## Applicant Rating

- Reviewers can rate applicants against configurable criteria.
- Ratings help compare candidates more consistently.
