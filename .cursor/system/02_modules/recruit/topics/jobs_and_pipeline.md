# Recruit Jobs and Pipeline

Use this topic for Recruit access, job posting rules, boards, and applicant status flow.

## Who Can Access Recruit

- Only users with Recruit access can see the Recruit module.
- Recruiter role or Admin role is required.
- Access can be restricted per job posting.
- Followers can follow an applicant and receive notifications without full Recruit access.

## Job Postings

- Jobs include title, department, location, description, and application form.
- Jobs must be `Published` to appear on the job board.
- Multiple job boards can be configured for different brands or locations.
- Job Posting Log tracks when and where a job was posted.
- Application forms can mix standard questions and custom questions from Question Library.

## Applicant Status Flow

1. `Applied`
2. Custom statuses such as Phone Screen, Interview, Final Round, or Offer
3. `Hired` or `Rejected`

Admins can add custom statuses and reorder them.
